Overview

Perched atop one of Ho Chi Minh City's most prestigious addresses, The Grand Sapphire Ballroom redefines luxury event experiences. With panoramic views of the glittering cityscape and an interior adorned with hand-crafted crystal chandeliers, this venue has become the premier choice for discerning hosts seeking an unforgettable celebration.

Our 3,500-square-meter pillarless ballroom seamlessly accommodates intimate gatherings of 100 to grand celebrations of 500 guests. Each event is orchestrated by our dedicated team of event specialists, ensuring every detail reflects your vision with precision and elegance.

From royal weddings to prestigious corporate galas, The Grand Sapphire Ballroom has hosted the city's most celebrated occasions, earning accolades for exceptional cuisine, impeccable service, and an ambiance that transforms moments into lasting memories.
